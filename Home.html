Zenless Zone Zero

Zenless Zone Zero is a fast-paced action RPG developed by HoYoverse. The game is set in a futuristic city called New Eridu, the last safe haven after a mysterious disaster created dangerous dimensions known as Hollows.

Players take on the role of a “Proxy,” guiding different combat agents as they explore Hollows, fight enemies, and uncover secrets behind the catastrophe.

The gameplay focuses on stylish real-time combat, smooth character-switching mechanics, and flashy combo attacks. With its urban fantasy setting, anime-inspired visuals, and energetic soundtrack, Zenless Zone Zero blends intense action with a modern, street-style aesthetic.
