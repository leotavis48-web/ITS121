How can we help you?

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my order?

You can track your order using the link sent to your email after purchase. If you haven't received a tracking number within 3 business days, please contact us.


What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn and in original packaging. Please note that shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer.


Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. International shipping times vary from 7 to 21 business days depending on your location.


How do I care for my zzz merch?

Most of our items are machine washable. Please check the care label on your specific item. We recommend washing in cold water and tumble drying on low heat to preserve the print.
